Cheesy Potato Bake

Description

I don’t know about you, but whenever I serve a potato bake to family or friends they’re so grateful. It’s as though I read their mind and have given them what they were secretly craving. This cheesy potato baake will have you requesting it for years to come.

Ingredients

  • 1kg washed white potatos
  • 250g bacon rashers, diced fine
  • 500ml thickened cream
  • 2 packets of Maggi cheese & bacon mix
  • 3 cups of shredded cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 180C/350F.
  2. Thinly slice the potatos and chop bacon then add to a baking dish.
  3. In a mixing bowl mix the thickened cream and Maggi mix's untill well combined. t]Then pour evenly over potatos in baking dish.
  4. Add chees evenly to the top then cover in aluminimum foil.
  5. Bake until potatos are soft then remove the foil and bake until cheese is golden.
  6. Set aside for 10 mins to cool slightly then serve.
